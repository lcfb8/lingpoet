Wander

A place to roam through coincidences — words that resemble one another without ancestry, borrowing, or intent.

Glad · Rooster

Two words. No shared root. No loan. No lineage. And yet — they meet, briefly, by sound alone.

Wander is for noticing these accidents: the way language sometimes folds in on itself and smiles.

Night · Knight

A darkness and an armor, colliding only by ear.

Sea · See

A body of water and an act of perception.

Flour · Flower

One feeds the body. One feeds the eye.
