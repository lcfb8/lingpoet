Explore words and sounds that randomly connect across languages

We're not interested in words with a similar etymological root or loan words. We're looking for the weird words, the ones that are the same purely through coincidence.

To start, let's look at some words in Norwegian and English that are spelled the same but mean completely different things. Note that as linguists, we are interested in all words, even those that may be considered slang, informal, or vulgar.
