Behind the words

This website is an amalgamation of effort, curiosity, admiration, and work. 

The linguistic data comes from <a href="https://www.kaikki.org>kaikki.org</a> (kaikki â€” [Finnish] all, everything, everyone), a digital archive and data mining group who have extracted millions of words, and the information about those words, from Wiktionary.

<a href="www.wiktionary.org">Wiktionary</a> is a free, collaborative, multilingual online dictionary which aims to describe all words of all languages using definitions and descriptions in English. Wiktionary is written by volunteer editors and hosted by the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia_Foundation">Wikimedia Foundation</a>. 

The word search was created by downloading kaikki's extract of all Wiktionary data and sorting through it (with AI help) to extract the words that match in spelling and pronunciation across languages. We filtered for words with divergent meanings to exclude instances where the match was due to a shared etymological root or a loan word. Our code can be found on <a href="www.github.com/lcfb8/lingpoet">GitHub</a>.
